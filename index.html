"Stellar Synergy" is a PC game designed for 2 players to connect their mobile device to the PC to control the mech. Each player has a different set of controls for example one walks forward the other backwards, and both players must press the Jump button in sync for the mech to Jump. Knock2Star

In this fast-paced Unreal Engine game, players compete to hunt down a glowing Star hidden on the map. Once a player captures the Star, they must hold onto it for 30 seconds while avoiding aggressive AI opponents that chase them relentlessly. Both players and AI can knock each other back in chaotic skirmishes. Scattered throughout the map are obstacles that provide speed boosts, slowdowns, and jump boosts, adding extra strategy and unpredictability to the chase.
